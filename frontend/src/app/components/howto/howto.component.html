How To Use LOGIN

Playmates lets you find mutual interests among people on campus. And it is simple to use!

The platform is ours, but the love-algorithm is yours! Join the Sexual Revolution, and enjoy!



Detailed Steps

  • Create a new account, through an phone confirmation, and then setting up a password.
  • Make sure you do not forget the password. If lost, your data cannot be recovered.
  • We do not send your plain password to the server even for login, so be assured :)
  • Log in to your account.
  • Select up to 5 of your interests.
  • These choices will NOT be visible to anyone else.
  • Lock and submit your choices! Remember, you cannot change your choices after this.
  • Your choices will receive an anonymous tip via their number urging them to add their own.
  • Check back regularly to receive hearts signifying how many people like you :)
  • Open the platform at midnight on 14th of February to know if one or more of your interests likes you back :)
  • Remember, if your interest does not like you back, he/she will NEVER know that you like them.
Good Luck!
