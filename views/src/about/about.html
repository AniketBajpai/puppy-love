Puppy Love helps you meet your crush, anonymously.

Express your interest, and both of you will be informed if the interest is mutual. Otherwise, no one will EVER know about your choice. No, not even the admin, i.e. us.

Why the change?

We can almost hear you say, why a NEW puppy love?

We hear you. This complete new version was built through the semesters, and has a HUGE number of improvements, which make it immensely more secure than the previous version.

  • If you do not match with someone, no one can ever know your choice, not even the administrator
  • Algorithm designed from the ground up to ensure the maximum level of trust possible theoretically
  • No information will ever leave your browser which lets the administrator or anyone else see your choices
  • Algorithm reviewed by professors and peers in terms of security and confidentiality
  • No need to use your CC password. You need to sign up and set a new password for puppy love by using your IITK Email address
  • Code in a high level language (and sane. PHP, we are looking at you) reduces chances of bugs and improves performance
